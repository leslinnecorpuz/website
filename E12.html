Yamaha Revstar 320 ICB

PHP10000.00

Inspired by Yamaha's classic motorcycles, this affordable solidbody electric guitar features a lightweight nato body for pronounced midrange tones and comfortable playability. Equipped with ceramic YGD humbuckers, it delivers a classic dual-coil tone, while the thin neck profile and sleek rosewood fretboard ensure effortless playing. The set-in neck design and aluminum bridge with a stopbar tailpiece enhance sustain, making it a versatile and stylish choice for players of all levels.
